Coping with COVID-19

The pandemic has limited the people to the outside world. In order to maintain good health in midst of pandemic, we, the advocates, encourage everyone to be dynamic on our respective homes and involve oneself with physical activities as a way of coping with the COVID-19.

Physical activities have several benefits. Including the body, mind and soul. It makes one active, mindful, alert, focused, and fit! Taking advantage of “staying at home”, we should engage ourselves to be productive and at the same time staying healthy. Learn more about the workout you can do at home!
